Welcome to the Peer Tutoring Center

"Since 2009, the NC Virtual Peer Tutoring Center has been helping students like you with all the features of online learning at the North Carolina Virtual Public School!  All Peer Tutors and Virtual Buddies have completed at least one NC Virtual course successfully and have a recommendation of an NC Virtual instructor. The PTC offers many services to help support NC Virtual students. Whether you need content-specific help with a particular concept in your course or you need a little extra motivation and help with time management, the PTC has you covered." (NCVirtual)
